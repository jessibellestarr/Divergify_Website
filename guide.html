Quick Start

  1. Open The Hub beta.
  2. Pick a sidekick personality that matches your current bandwidth.
  3. Add one tiny task and run one timer.
  4. Switch on Shades for low-stim, or Tin Foil Hat for strict privacy behavior.

FAQ

I still see old sidekick labels. Why?

The app can cache old files. Close and reopen once, then refresh. If needed, clear site data for divergify.app and reload.

Is this mobile-ready and can I pin an icon?

Yes. Open The Hub beta on your phone and choose Add to Home Screen from your browser menu.

Where is my data stored?

Core workspace data is local-first on your device unless you explicitly choose integration features.

Can I switch sidekicks later?

Yes. Open Sidekicks and switch anytime.
