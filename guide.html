Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Divergify?

Divergify is a task and regulation app built for neurodivergent brains. It reduces overwhelm, lowers decision fatigue, and helps you take action without spiraling.

It focuses on clarity, speed, and emotional safety instead of productivity guilt.

2. Who is Divergify for?

Divergify is designed for people with ADHD, autism, anxiety, executive dysfunction, overwhelm, and burnout.

If traditional planners feel like punishment, this is built for you.

3. How is Divergify different from other productivity apps?

Most apps give you lists. Divergify gives you one focus at a time, energy-based task suggestions, cognitive load tagging, built-in emotional regulation tools, and a Sidekick that nudges instead of shames.

It is structured to reduce friction, not increase pressure.

4. What is the Sidekick?

The Sidekick is a short-form AI guide inside the app. It gives grounding prompts, micro nudges, pattern interrupts, and small dopamine wins.

It is designed to be brief, supportive, and non-overwhelming.

5. Does Divergify replace therapy?

No. Divergify is a tool for daily structure and regulation. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace professional care.

6. Is my data private?

Yes. Divergify stores your data locally on your device by default. We do not sell personal information.

Privacy and low-friction design are core principles of the system.

7. What is the Sticky feature?

Sticky is a quick capture tool. You tap once, write the thought, then send it to either Task Inbox or Ideas Vault.

It prevents mental clutter from turning into chaos piles.

8. What are cognitive load tags?

Tasks are labeled Light, Medium, or Heavy. This helps you choose tasks based on energy instead of guilt.

Your brain state matters more than the size of the task.

9. What if I feel overwhelmed?

Divergify includes one-tap grounding tools: breathing resets, pattern interrupts, and sensory-friendly screens.

The app is built to calm first, then move.

10. Is Divergify only for kids?

No. It is built for teens and adults navigating modern cognitive demands across school, work, and life.

11. Will there be premium features?

Yes. Premium supports advanced features and helps fund reinvestment into neurodivergent youth programs.

Core emotional regulation and task clarity remain accessible.

12. Is Divergify about making people normal?

No. Divergify is built around the idea that divergent minds are valuable.

The goal is stability and structure, not conformity.
