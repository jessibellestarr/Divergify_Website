App Build

Divergify is being built to help you think, plan, and execute without fighting how your brain actually works. No forced routines. No shame loops. No pretending motivation fixes structural problems.

The goal is simple: reduce friction so intelligence can do its job. The Hub is the center. Takota is optional guidance. Low interference modes keep your nervous system inside the window where you can function.
