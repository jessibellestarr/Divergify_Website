What Divergify is

Divergify is a neurodivergent ops center. Not a planner that shames you. Not a “just try harder” machine. A tool that adapts to the way you actually think.

What we believe

  • Neurodivergence is not a defect. It is an advantage that needs the right interface.
  • Structure should feel like relief, not punishment.
  • Progress beats perfection. Data beats shame.

Behavioral science, but with consent

Yes, we use research backed behavioral tools like micro commitments, positive reinforcement, pattern interruption, emotional reflection, identity cues, and future self alignment.

No, we do not manipulate you, punish you, guilt trip you, sell your data, or weaponize your tendencies.

You already have the engine. Divergify helps you steer it.

Our secret weapon: the AI Sidekick

The app includes an adaptive Sidekick you choose. Sarcastic gremlin. Soft guardian. Military coach. Calm anchor. Whatever fits your brain that day.

It doesn’t lecture you. It doesn’t guilt you. It helps you name what’s happening, pick one next step, and move.

Not therapy. Not clinical. Just honest support.

Privacy is not a feature. It’s a value.

Divergify is built to honor autonomy and privacy. To the point of adding a literal Tin Foil Hat Mode.

The mission

  • Stability before wealth
  • Reinvestment into neurodivergent youth
  • Support for teachers and parents
  • Modern tools for minds that do not fit factory systems

The world isn’t built for us yet. Divergify helps bridge that gap. We don’t fix your brain. We fix the tools.
