The Hub

The Hub is where you take messy mental traffic and turn it into a sequence you can actually run without burning out. Not motivation. Not vibes. Execution.

Your brain already detects patterns and threats before you can explain them. Divergify doesn’t “fix” that. It builds structure around it and lowers the friction so intent can turn into action.

Friction Mapping

Beat your baseline

Progress is measured against your baseline, not against a stranger’s highlight reel.
