The Hub

The Hub is the execution layer. Not motivation, not vibes, not a planner cosplay. It is where you take messy mental traffic and turn it into a sequence you can actually run without burning out.

The core idea is simple: your brain already detects patterns and threats before you can explain them. Divergify does not try to “fix” that. It builds structure around it, then reduces friction so your intent can cross the gap into action.

Mode Matching

Pick tools that match your current state, not your ideal fantasy version of yourself.

Friction Mapping

Identify the exact point tasks fail, then remove that obstacle before you ask for willpower.

Baseline Beat

Progress is measured against your baseline, not against a stranger’s highlight reel.
