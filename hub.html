The Hub

Integrated planning and life management for neurodivergent minds

The Hub is where you take messy mental traffic and turn it into a sequence you can actually run without burning out. Not motivation. Not vibes. Execution.

Your brain already detects patterns and threats before you can explain them. Divergify doesn’t “fix” that. It builds structure around it and lowers the friction so intent can turn into action.

Mode Matching

Pick tools that match your current state, not your ideal fantasy version of yourself.

Friction Mapping

Identify the exact point tasks fail, then remove that obstacle before you ask for willpower.

Beat your baseline

Progress is measured against your baseline, not against a stranger’s highlight reel.

The App + Beta

Divergify helps neurodivergent brains turn chaos into momentum with adaptive tools, honest support, and AI that works the way you think.

Core workspace data stays local by default. Any off-device AI or cloud feature is opt-in and user-controlled.

The Hub is for execution support and planning; it is not a medical diagnosis or treatment product.
