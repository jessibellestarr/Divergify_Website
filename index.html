Why this structure works

Neurodivergent attention follows relevance, safety, and capacity.

When a task feels unclear or costly, the brain protects itself by slowing down or disengaging.

The fix is not pressure. It is better conditions.

For deeper context, check out Field Notes.

What you can expect

  • Calm, predictable design.
  • Local first defaults with privacy built in.
  • Explicit consent before off-device processing.
  • Support that preserves your autonomy.

This isn’t productivity theater. It’s a workspace for competence.

Divergify supports effort. It doesn’t replace it.

We don’t use shame, manipulation, surveillance, artificial urgency, or deceit to force output.

We strive to reduce unnecessary friction and support thinking when it falters.

Who this is for

  • People who think in systems, not steps.
  • People who do well in complexity but stall in rigid workflows.
  • People who deal with executive dysfunction, RSD, or hyperfocus and are tired of pretending those aren’t factors.
