What we build

Divergify is an environment that supports execution without forcing you to mask.

We design for the real costs of attention, working memory, and emotional load.

That means smaller steps, clearer scaffolding, and fewer triggers.

Why this structure works

Neurodivergent attention follows relevance, novelty, and safety. When a task feels unclear or costly, the brain protects itself by drifting or shutting down.

Research on ADHD, autism, and trauma shows that executive load rises fast when expectations outpace capacity. The fix is not willpower. It is better conditions.

We build those conditions: clearer cues, smaller steps, reduced sensory noise, and permission to recalibrate.
