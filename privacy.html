Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information

Effective date: this policy is effective when posted. It explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Overview

Divergify is built with consent as the default. We do not sell personal information. We do not build attention traps. If optional analytics are added, the Tin Foil Hat Mode toggle disables them.

We do not sell your data. Some third-party services and browsers may collect their own data outside our control.

Information we collect

We collect information you choose to provide, such as when you contact us.

  • Contact details (name, email, and message content).
  • Support details you include in your message.

We may collect limited technical data for basic site operations and security.

  • IP address, browser type, device information, and access timestamps.
  • Referrer and page request data.

If you support Divergify through Kofi, payment details are handled by Kofi. We receive confirmation and any public profile information you choose to share there.

How we use information

  • Provide, maintain, and secure the site.
  • Respond to messages and support requests.
  • Improve content and usability.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

How we share information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only as needed to operate the site, comply with law, protect our rights, or with your explicit consent.

Cookies and analytics

We may use limited cookies for essential functionality. If analytics are enabled, they are optional and respect the Tin Foil Hat Mode toggle by disabling tracking when it is on.

Your choices

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your contact messages.
  • Opt out of non-essential analytics by enabling Tin Foil Hat Mode if available.
  • Stop communicating at any time.

Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purposes described here, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information. No system is perfectly secure, so please avoid sending sensitive information through email.

Children's privacy

The site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us to remove it.

International access

If you access the site from outside our operating region, your information may be processed where we or our service providers operate.

Changes and contact

We may update this policy periodically. The latest version will be posted here. For questions, contact chaoscontrol@divergify.app.
